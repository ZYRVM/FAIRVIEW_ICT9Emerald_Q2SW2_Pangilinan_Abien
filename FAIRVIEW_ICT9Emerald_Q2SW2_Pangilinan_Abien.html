Types of Cat Breeds

Abyssinian Cat

Abyssinian Cats are quite investigative of their environment. They're also very intelligent and inquisitive. They are playful too because they like grabbing things that they're interested in. That's they're entitled to the nickname, "Aby-grabbys".

Bengal Cat

Bengal Cats are very curious and confident-looking cats. They're also fond of people because of their playful attitude and personality. However, there are some several defects about this breed but it can improve as it ages.
